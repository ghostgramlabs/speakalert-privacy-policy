# Privacy Policy for SpeakAlert

Effective Date: February 2026

SpeakAlert respects your privacy. This app is designed to work fully offline and does not collect or share personal data.

## Information Collection and Use
SpeakAlert does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

- Voice recordings are stored locally on your device
- Text reminders are stored locally on your device
- No data is uploaded to any server

## Permissions
SpeakAlert uses the following permissions:

- Microphone: Required to record voice reminders
- Notifications: Required to deliver reminders
- Exact Alarms: Required for precise reminder timing

These permissions are used only for core app functionality.

## Data Sharing
SpeakAlert does not share any user data with third parties.

## Data Security
All data remains on the user's device. SpeakAlert does not use cloud storage, analytics, or tracking.
