# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 2026

SpeakAlert respects your privacy. This app is designed to work primarily offline and does not collect or share personal data.

---

## **Information Collection and Use**

SpeakAlert does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to its own servers.

- Voice recordings and reminder data are stored locally on your device  
- No data is uploaded to SpeakAlert servers  

If Android device backup is enabled, some app data may be backed up to your Google account by the Android system.

---

## **Permissions**

SpeakAlert uses the following permissions only for core app functionality:

- **Microphone**: Required to record voice reminders  
- **Notifications**: Required to deliver reminders  
- **Exact Alarms (USE_EXACT_ALARM)**: Required for precise reminder timing  
- **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED**: Required to reschedule reminders after device restart  
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE**: Required to run reminder playback in the foreground  
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK**: Required for audio playback while the app is not in the foreground  
- **WAKE_LOCK**: Ensures reminders can play reliably when the device is locked  
- **VIBRATE**: Enables vibration alerts for reminders  

These permissions are used only to support reminder functionality and are not used for tracking or data collection.

---

## **Foreground Service**

SpeakAlert may use a foreground service to play reminder sounds reliably, especially when the device is locked. This service runs only when a reminder is active and stops automatically afterward.
